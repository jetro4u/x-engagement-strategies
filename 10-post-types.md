## 3. Symmetric Comparisons

This post is great to show transformations.
You show where you were and where you are right now.
The double list format makes it aesthetically pleasing.
I call this Aesthetic Writing because it makes your writing look better and it improves the readability (which leads to more growth).

Example:

HOOK.  I am an addict

LIST 1. 2020
           - Weed
           - Games
           - Nicotine

LIST 2. 2023
           - Fitness 
           - Writing 
           - Business 

LESSON. Find better addiction

## 5. Split Sentences

This post breaks up 1 sentence into 2.

This helps to create intrigue.

It forces people to read the second line to get the insight.

EXAMPLE:

PART 1. If you don't sacrifice your goal 

PART 2. Your goal become the sacrifice

## 8. Triad Structure

The triad structure shows how 3 things are connected.

Groups of 3 create rhythm.

So this post makes it more satisfying for people to read.

EXAMPLE
